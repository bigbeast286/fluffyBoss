Pet Vaccination Schedule

Dog Vaccination Schedule

Disease 1st Vaccine Follow-up
Canine Distemper 6-8 Weeks Every 3-4 weeks until 14-16 weeks. Booster at 1 year then every 3 years
Infectious Canine Hepatitis 6-8 Weeks Given with combination vaccines
Canine Parvovirus 6-8 Weeks Given with combination vaccines
Rabies 3 Months Second dose after 1 year then every 1-3 years

Cat Vaccination Schedule

Disease 1st Vaccine Follow-up
Feline Panleukopenia 6-8 Weeks Every 3-4 weeks until 16 weeks
Feline Calicivirus 6-8 Weeks Booster every 3 years
Feline Viral Rhinotracheitis 6-8 Weeks Booster every 3 years
Rabies 12 Weeks Booster every 1-3 years
